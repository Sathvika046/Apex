Folk Dances In INDIA

Bharatanatyam is a classical Indian dance from Tamil Nadu.

Kathak is a classical dance that originated in Uttar Pradesh.

Kuchipudi originates from the village of Kuchipudi in Andhra Pradesh.

Manipuri dance originates from the state of Manipur.

Odissi is a major classical dance from Odisha.

Sattriya is a classical Indian dance from Assam.

Kathakali is a classical dance-drama from Kerala.

Mohiniyattam is a graceful classical dance from Kerala.

Garba is a popular folk dance of Gujarat.

Ghoomar is a traditional folk dance of Rajasthan.

Lavani is a traditional folk dance of Maharashtra known for its powerful rhythm.

Bhangra is a lively and energetic folk dance from Punjab.
