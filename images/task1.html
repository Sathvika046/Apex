Type Of Folk Dances In INDIA

Bharatanatyam is a classical Indian dance form originating from Tamil Nadu.

Kathak is a classical Indian dance form that originated in Uttar Pradesh.

Kuchipudi is a classical Indian dance form originating from the village of Kuchipudi in Andhra Pradesh.

Manipuri dance is a classical Indian dance form originating from the state of Manipur.

Odissi is a major Indian classical dance form that originated in the state of Odisha.

Sattriya is a classical Indian dance form originating from Assam,India.

Kathakali is a classical Indian dance-drama, originating from Kerala.

Mohiniyattam is a classical Indian dance form originating from Kerala.

Garba is a vibrant, ritualistic folk dance from Gujarat.

Ghoomar, a traditional Indian dance of Rajasthan.
